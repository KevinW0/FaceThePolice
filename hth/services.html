Why FInancial Literacy?

Financial literacy is a topic that is often overlooked and that can have negative long-term implications. The Young Investor Program Canada provides a platform to educate youth on the topic of financial literacy and investing for their futures.

Young Investor Program Canada traces its roots to Light up a Future 2013 where participants were challenged to “Innovate, Create, and Educate”. 

Akaash and Rohit Bali, now Commerce graduates of the Sauder School of Business at the University of British Columbia, impressed the judges by pitching a program which would ultimately become Young Investor Program Canada. With their success, they obtained valuable seed funding to launch the program.

Now entering its third year, Young Investor Program Canada has impacted the lives of over 250 secondary school students across Canada. In April of 2015, Young Investor Program Canada was awarded the Nova Star Award which recognizes organizations that execute their visions in a creative and unique way. 

We are slated to grow even bigger in the upcoming 2018 year, with more excitement to come. Join us as we strive to establish the next generation of this city and country’s financial leaders!

VANCOUVER

FOUNDING LOCATION

Young Investor Program Vancouver was established in 2012, by Akaash Bali, Rohit Bali, James Yue, and Joseph Ip. Hosted at the UBC Sauder School of Business, the program has now grown from 35 students to 100 students in three years. The program continues to engage its students in educational and interactive workshops, competitions, and more. 

Toronto

In 2013, the Young Investor Program expanded to Toronto and is building on the success enjoyed over the past years. 

CALGARY

We are excited to announce that 2018 is the first year YIP is running a program in Calgary! If you are interested in helping the Young Investor Program expand, send us an email at info@yipcanada.com

Our vision

We are determined to educate students on the importance of financial literacy and help them create a strong foundation to make sound financial decisions in the future. We believe in the importance of financial literacy as a powerful tool that enables our students to become knowledgeable, global citizens. 

We seek to expand to various cities across Canada throughout the next few years and create a national platform for financial literacy education.
